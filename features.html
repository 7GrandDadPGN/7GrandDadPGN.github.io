Combat.

The Combat modules in Vape are designed to give you small, unnoticeable advantages in combat. With the right settings, these modules will help you crush your opponent completely undetected.

  • AimAssist Smoothly aims to close range targets
  • AutoClicker Automatically clicks for you while holding down the attack button
  • Reach Increases your attack distance
  • HitSelect Interrupts attack speed to improve movement speed and more
  • Velocity Reduces knockback
  • Sprint Automatically activates sprinting for you
  • WTap Automatically WTaps for you
  • SilentAura Convenience of Killaura, but as undetectable as AutoClicker and Aimassist

Render.

Whether it be Players, Blocks, or anything else; Render modules provide you with the ability to see things you normally wouldn't, with ease.

  • Search Renders an outline of blocks through walls
  • NameTags Renders name plates of entities through walls
  • Tracers Draws a line to entities on screen
  • StorageESP Draws an ESP around storage blocks
  • ESP Draws a box around entities
  • AntiDebuff Removes debuff effects
  • Arrows Draws arrows to players outside of your field of view
  • Chams Renders players through walls
  • Fullbright Removes darkness
  • ItemESP Renders tag on dropped items
  • Projectiles Shows trajectories of projectiles in the air
  • PropHunt Shows where hidden props are
  • SpawnerFinder Renders name tags on mob spawners
  • Trajectories Shows the path of where your held projectile item will land

Utility.

For tedious and time sensitive tasks, Utility modules automate things quickly and efficiently.

  • Scaffold Assists with bridging
  • AutoPearl Aims and throws a pearl at an enemies pearl trajectory
  • Panic Disables all currently enabled modules
  • FakeLag Delays packets, simulating lag
  • Blink Chokes packets until disabled
  • BackTrack Lags you at certain times to increase attack distance

World.

World modules give you the ability to interact with the world more efficiently.

  • AutoTool Automatically swaps your hotbar slot to the appropriate tool
  • ChestSteal Takes items upon opening a chest
  • FastPlace Lowers the block place delay
  • FreeCam Allows you to move through the world freely without moving your actual player position
  • MLG Automatically places water under you if you fall

Inventory.

These modules help you manage your inventory

  • AutoArmor Automatically equips armor as needed
  • AutoHeal Automatically uses healing items
  • InvCleaner Cleans unnecessary items out of your inventory
  • ThrowDebuff Throws debuff potions
  • Throwpot Throws or consumes healing items upon using keybind
  • InventoryManager Manages your inventory
  • ArmorSwitch ArmorSwitch Switches between armor sets
  • Refill Refills your hotbar with healing items

Besides just the modules, Vape has several other unique and convenient features.
